Felis Catus: Cat

Species Traits:

  • Can hear sounds too faint or too high in frequency for human ears
  • Very social species
  • Can see in near darkness

Conservation Status: Domesticated





Felis Chaus: Jungle Cat

Species Traits:

  • Uniformly sandy, reddish-brown or grey fur without spots
  • Mostly solitary in nature
  • Kittens begin to catch their own prey around sixth months and leave their mother

Conservation Status: Least Concern





Felis Silvestris: European Wildcat

Species Traits:

  • Predominantly nocturnal
  • Striped fur and dark dorsal bands
  • Have proportionally larger teeth compared to the domestic cat

Conservation Status: Least Concern





Felis Nigripes: Black-Footed Cat

Species Traits:

  • The smallest cat in Africa
  • Despite its name, only the pads and underparts of the cat's feet are black
  • Have extreme bravery (A San legends claims it can kill a giraffe by piercing its jugular

Conservation Status: Vulnerable





Felis Margarita: Sand Cat

Species Traits:

  • Well adapted to the extremes of a desert environment and both extremely hot and cold conditions
  • Has much greater hearing capacity and sensitivity than domestic cats
  • Very powerful bite

Conservation Status: Least Concern





Felis Bieti: Chinese Mountain Cat

Species Traits:

  • Tip of the tail is black
  • Sand colored fur with dark guard hairs
  • Very long and heavy compared to others in the genus

Conservation Status: Vulnerable
